## Overview

**Inbox Lens** brings AI analysis to your email inbox. It connects to your mailbox, reads threads, and surfaces what matters — summaries, action items, and smart categorization — so you can get through your inbox faster.

## Tech Stack

- **Python** — core analysis engine
- **LLM API** — summarization and extraction
- **Gmail / IMAP** — email access
